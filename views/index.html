One Tap Man, a hero for fun

Tap to save the city

Through rigorous training everyday of: 100 push-ups, 100 sit-ups, 100 squats and a 10km run, our hero has developed the power to destroy anything with one tap.

Giant meteors are raining down on The Bay Area! Keep the area safe by destroying everything in the sky. The grateful citizens of The Bay will give you money for every one you destroy. Smaller meteors are harder to hit so they're worth more money than larger ones.
